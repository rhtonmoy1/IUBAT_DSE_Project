About DSE

DSE foundation

Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE) is a public limited company. It is formed and managed under Company Act 1994, Security and Exchange Commission Act 1993, Security and Exchange Commission Regulation 1994, and Security Exchange (Inside Trading) regulation 1994. The issued capital of this company is Tk.

BPM System

DSE BPM system is designed for digital submission and E-Governance. The modules in this system are Company Fundamental, News & PSI, Declaration, Quarterly Information, Periodical Submission, Corporate Action and Data Archiving. These actions can be performed efficiently from both Issuer and DSE end using this system.
